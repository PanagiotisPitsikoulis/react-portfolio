## Overview

A full booking flow with calendar availability, location search, and secure payments.

## Highlights

- Search with multi-criteria filters and map viewport syncing
- Availability service backed by Postgres + Prisma
- Stripe Payments with receipt emails and refunds

## Outcomes

- Search latency P95 under 150ms
- Refund processing automated; reduced manual ops by 80%
