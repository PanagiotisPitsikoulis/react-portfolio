## Overview

A modern e‑commerce experience with optimized product pages, server actions for cart operations, and Stripe Checkout.

## Highlights

- GraphQL-backed product catalog with ISR
- Server Actions for cart and checkout flows
- Image CDN and facet filters for fast browsing

## Outcomes

- +18% conversion vs. monolith baseline
- P75 LCP under 1.2s on mobile
