# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how this website (the “Site”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit or interact with it. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Personal Information you voluntarily provide via forms (e.g., name, email, message content).
- Usage Data automatically collected (e.g., IP address, browser type, device, pages visited, referral URLs, and timestamps) for analytics and security.
- Cookies and similar technologies that help remember preferences and measure Site performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To operate and improve the Site’s functionality and reliability.
- To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
- To analyze usage patterns and optimize content and performance.
- To protect against spam, fraud, and abuse and to maintain security.

## 3. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use:

- Essential cookies for core functionality (e.g., session, security).
- Preference cookies to remember settings like theme.
- Analytics cookies to understand engagement and improve UX.

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may impact the Site’s functionality.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by law. Non-personal and aggregated analytics data may be retained for longer to help improve the Site.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the Site (e.g., hosting, analytics) under appropriate safeguards. We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

## 6. International Transfers

If you access the Site from outside the hosting region, your information may be processed in other jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws. We take reasonable steps to protect your information consistent with this Policy.

## 7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR/EEA)

If you are in the EEA or a similar jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Data portability, where applicable
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the Contact page.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review the policies of any third-party sites you visit.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date indicates the latest revision. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

## 12. Contact

For any questions or requests regarding this Policy, please reach out via the Site’s Contact page.
